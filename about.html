About Us

Leading ICT and media services provider since 2025

Welcome to Christopher Stationary & Library Movies

Founded in 2025 september by Zacharia Christopher Sugilo, we have grown from a small stationary shop to a comprehensive ICT and media services provider. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the community at lagana kishapu.

We specialize in providing a wide range of services including movie burning, printing, phone repairs, TV installation, and online application assistance. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional service to every customer.
