Our History

Founder Spotlight

Zacharia Christopher Sugilo

Founded Christopher Stationary with a vision to provide comprehensive ICT services to the local community.

With a background in information technology and a passion for entrepreneurship, Zacharia started the business with a small stationary shop. His vision was to create a one-stop solution for ICT and media needs in the community.

Under his leadership, the business has grown from a single shop to a comprehensive service provider with multiple departments and a team of dedicated professionals.
