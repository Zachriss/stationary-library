Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your payment methods?

We accept cash, mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), and bank transfers.

Do you offer home service for TV installation?

Yes, we provide professional home installation services for all major TV providers including Azam TV, DSTV, Startimes, and Zuku.

How long does phone repair take?

Most smartphone repairs are completed within 2-4 hours. Complex repairs may take 24-48 hours.

Can I order movies online?

Yes, you can place movie orders through our WhatsApp number or by calling our office.

Do you provide backup for my data during phone repair?

We offer optional data backup service for an additional fee. We recommend backing up your data before any repair.

What is your refund policy?

We offer refunds for undelivered services within 7 days. Service fees are non-refundable once work has commenced.
